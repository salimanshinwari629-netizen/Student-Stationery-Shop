Our Story

Students Stationary Shop was started with one simple goal: to make quality supplies affordable and accessible for every student in Landikotal.

We know how hard it can be to find good stationery, chargers, handsfree, sports gear, or SIM cards locally. So we built a shop that brings everything under one roof — and delivers orders straight through WhatsApp so you never have to wait long.

Our Values
Honesty
We price fairly and never hide fees or charges
Quality
Only products we'd use ourselves make it to the shelf
Speed
Fast confirmation and quick delivery via WhatsApp
Community
Supporting students of Landikotal is our mission
